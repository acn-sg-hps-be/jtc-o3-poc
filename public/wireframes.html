Option 1 — Phased stepper + parallel sub-tabs recommended

The 4 reviews collapse into ONE “Review” step; the 4 acknowledgements into ONE “Acknowledgement” step. Inside, a tab per discipline ((a) always, b/c/d when active). Tabs imply no order = parallel. Top-level stepper ≈ 8 steps.

Option 2 — Workflow board (status dashboard, no stepper)

All sections as status cards grouped by phase columns; parallel reviews stacked under one column. Click a card to fill it, in any order. Best mirror of concurrent multi-party approval.
